|{{Indent}}This is the science of fossil study. Make a skill roll to recognize fossils or to deduce an organism's habitat, structure, etc. from fossil evidence. A successful roll - with equipment modifiers (p. 345) for lab facilities - identifies the approximate age of a fossil

{{Indent}}You must specialize:

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Micropaleontology:&lt;/i&gt; The study of fossils too small to be seen with the naked eye. This skill requires a lab

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Paleoanthropology:&lt;/i&gt; The study of human fossils and tools, and the relation of primitive tribes to their habitats. Also defaults to Anthropology-2

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Paleobotany:&lt;/i&gt; The study of vegetable fossils

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Paleozoology:&lt;/i&gt; The study of prehistoric animals from fossilized bones, food, feces, footprints, etc. These specialties default to one another at -2
