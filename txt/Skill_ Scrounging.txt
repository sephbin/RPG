|{{Indent}}This is the ability to find, salvage, or improvise useful items that others can't locate. Each attempt takes an hour. You do not necessarily steal your booty; you just locate it - somehow - and then acquire it by any means necessary. Note that if you find something that is &quot;nailed down,&quot; you must decide how to try to get it (which might require a roll on another skill)
