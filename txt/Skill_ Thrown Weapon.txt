{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Shuriken:&lt;/i&gt; Any sort of hiltless blade, notably shuriken (&quot;ninja stars&quot;). &lt;i&gt;Default:&lt;/i&gt; Throwing-2

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Spear:&lt;/i&gt; Any sort of spear, javelin, etc. &lt;i&gt;Defaults:&lt;/i&gt; Spear Thrower-4 or Thrown Weapon (Harpoon)-2
