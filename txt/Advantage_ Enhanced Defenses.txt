|{{Indent}}You are unusually adept at evading attacks! This may be due to careful observation of your foe, focusing chi, or anything else that fits your background.

{{Indent}}There are three versions:

{{Indent}}Enhanced Block: You have +1 to your Block score with either Cloak or Shield skill. You must specialize in one particular Block defense. 5 points.

{{Indent}}Enhanced Dodge: You have +1 to your Dodge score. 15 points.

{{Indent}}Enhanced Parry: You have +1 to your Parry score. You may take this advantage for bare hands (5 points), for any one Melee Weapon skill (5 points), or for all parries (10 points). 5 or 10 points.
