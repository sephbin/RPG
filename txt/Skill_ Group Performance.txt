|{{Indent}}This is the ability to arrange a performance and direct a group of performers in its execution - in rehearsal, in a studio, or before a live audience. A successful roll means the performance is pleasing. You must specialize in a particular performing art. All specialties have prerequisites: the specific skills listed below, plus any one of Diplomacy, Intimidation, or Leadership

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Choreography:&lt;/i&gt; The ability to instruct and lead a group of dancers. &lt;i&gt;Prerequisite:&lt;/i&gt; Dancing. &lt;i&gt;Default:&lt;/i&gt; Dancing-2

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Conducting:&lt;/i&gt; The ability to coordinate a group of musicians. Choirs, swing bands, symphony orchestras, etc. are different familiarities; see Familiarity (p. 169). &lt;i&gt;Prerequisites:&lt;/i&gt; Any two Musical Instrument skills, or one Musical Instrument and Singing. &lt;i&gt;Defaults:&lt;/i&gt; Musical Instrument-2 or Singing-2

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Directing:&lt;/i&gt; The ability to direct a group of actors. Film, opera, television, and theater are different familiarities. &lt;i&gt;Prerequisite:&lt;/i&gt; Performance. &lt;i&gt;Default:&lt;/i&gt; Performance-5

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Fight Choreography:&lt;/i&gt; Similar to Choreography, but for Stage Combat (p. 222) instead of Dancing. &lt;i&gt;Prerequisite:&lt;/i&gt; Stage Combat. &lt;i&gt;Default:&lt;/i&gt; Stage Combat-2
