{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Copper:&lt;/i&gt; Copper itself and its alloys, including brass and bronze. Traditionally, a smith who worked with these metals was called a &quot;brownsmith.&lt;i&gt;&quot; Default:&lt;/i&gt; Jeweler-4

{{Indent}}&lt;i&gt;Iron:&lt;/i&gt; The skill of being a blacksmith. Also covers steel, at tech levels where it exists

{{Indent}}Lead and Tin: Any of the softer, &quot;white&quot; metals, including alloys such as pewter. The traditional name for such a smith was &quot;whitesmith.&lt;i&gt;&quot; Default:&lt;/i&gt; Jeweler-4

{{Indent}}These specialties default to one another at -4
