|{{Indent}}You have extraordinary reactions, and are rarely surprised for more than a moment. You get +1 to all active defense rolls (see Defending, p. 374), &quot;+1 to Fast-Draw skill, and +2 to Fright Checks (see Fright Checks, p. 360). You never &quot;freeze&quot; in a surprise situation, and get +6 on all IQ rolls to  wake up, or to recover from surprise or mental &quot;stun.&quot; Your side gets +1 on initiative rolls to avoid a surprise attack - +2 if you are the leader. For details, see Surprise Attacks and Initiative (p. 393).

{{Indent}}Combat Reflexes is included in Enhanced Time Sense (p. 52). If you have ETS, you cannot also take Combat Reflexes.
