{{Indent}}Chummy: You react to others at +2 most of the time. When alone, you are unhappy and distracted, and suffer a -1 penalty to IQ-based skills. -5 points.

{{Indent}}Gregarious: You usually react to others at +4. You are miserable when alone, and use IQ-based skills at -2 - or at -1 if in a group of four or less. -10 points.
