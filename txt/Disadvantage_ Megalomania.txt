|{{Indent}}You believe you are a superman, that you have been chosen for some great task, or that you are destined to conquer. You must choose a grand goal - most often conquest or the completion of some fantastic task. You must let nothing stand between you and this goal.

{{Indent}}You may attract followers with Fanaticism, but nobody else enjoys hearing you talk about your brilliance and great plans. Young or naive characters, and fanatics looking for a new cause, react to you at +2; others will react at -2.

{{Indent}}This is a better disadvantage for NPCs than it is for PCs.
