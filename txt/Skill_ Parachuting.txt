|{{Indent}}This is the ability to survive a parachute jump. Roll once per jump. Failure could mean anything from drifting off course to panic that makes you drop your gear (GM's option). A critical failure means the chute did not open or was fatally fouled (see Falling, p. 431). For a jump under bad conditions, make a second roll on landing - for instance, to survive an &quot;ankle-breaker&quot; landing without injury, or to dodge trees on the way down. Make an IQ-based roll to pack a parachute
