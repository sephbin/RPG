SEE INVISIBLE

15 points

You can see objects or individuals that are normally invisible. Buy this advantage separately for each kind of invisibility.
True Sight: Your vision can penetrate all deceptions related to the type of invisibility you can foil, including camouflage, disguises, and illusions. For instance, True Sight would allow See Invisible (Magical) to defeat magical illusions and the Blur spell (p. B250) as easily as magical invisibility. +50%.
